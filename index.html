Let's Talk About Pollution

Together, we can make our planet cleaner and happier!

What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into our environment. It affects air, water, and land, and can harm humans, animals, and plants.

Types of Pollution

  • Air Pollution: Smoke from factories, vehicles, and burning waste.
  • Water Pollution: Dumping waste into rivers, lakes, and oceans.
  • Land Pollution: Littering, plastic waste, and chemicals in soil.

How Can We Help?

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle.
  • Use public transport or cycle.
  • Plant more trees.
  • Avoid single-use plastics.
